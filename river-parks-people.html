Ambassadors

Memphis River Parks Ambassadors are volunteers with a passion for the riverfront who commit to advocating for, raising funds and volunteering to support the riverfront. The Partnership accepts applications for ambassadors bi-annually. Sign up here to be notified of the next application window.
